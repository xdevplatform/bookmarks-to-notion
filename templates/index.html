Welcome to the Twitter Bookmarks to Notion app. This app lets you import your Bookmarked Tweets to a Notion page.

To get started, we'll need you to authorize bookmarks-to-notion app to access your bookmarks on Twitter using OAuth 2.0. Learn more information about OAuth 2.0

Here's how it works:

  1. We will redirect you to Twitter.com for you to authorize our app.
  2. While at Twitter.com, you will authorize bookmarks-to-notion to access your bookmarks. Upon authorization, we will get your recent 100 bookmarks.
  3. Next we will direct you to the Notion authorization flow. When you have authorized this app to create a Notion page for you, we will create a page with your Twitter Bookmarks and provide you the URL for that page.
